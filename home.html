Genres

ROCK

Rock music is characterized by a strong beat, simple chord progressions, and often revolves around the electric guitar. It originated in the 1950s and has since evolved into various subgenres.

CLASSICAL

Classical music is a broad term that usually refers to the formal musical tradition of the Western world, encompassing a variety of styles from the medieval period to the present day. It is known for its complex structures and orchestral arrangements.

COUNTRY MUSIC

Country music originated in the southern United States in the 1920s. It often features ballads and dance tunes with simple forms, folk lyrics, and harmonies generally accompanied by instruments such as banjos, fiddles, harmonicas, and guitars.
